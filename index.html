About Us

Braam Fashion Week is a celebration of creativity, innovation, and style that brings together emerging and established designers from across South Africa. Our mission is to showcase the vibrant fashion landscape of Johannesburg while providing a platform for local talent to shine on both national and international stages. Founded with a vision to transform Braamfontein into a fashion hub, we curate unforgettable experiences that blend traditional African aesthetics with contemporary design. From runway shows to designer exhibitions, networking events to fashion workshops, Braam Fashion Week is where the future of African fashion comes alive.
